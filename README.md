Instructions:
When the fish gauge appears, reel in the fish by clicking when the fish arrow is in the green zone.

Each time you reel the fish in, you deal one damage to the fish. Deal enough damage, and you can catch the fish!

Each fish is worth a specific amount of money. Gain enough money to buy boat upgrades in the shop to fish in deeper water with more expensive fish.

Good Luck...
